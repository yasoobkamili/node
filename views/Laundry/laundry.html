Laundry FAQs

Answers to common laundry questions

How does the pickup and delivery work?

Simply schedule your pickup through our app or website. We'll arrive at your chosen time to collect your laundry. After professional cleaning, we'll return your fresh clothes within 24-48 hours. Delivery is always free!

Do you offer eco-friendly cleaning options?

Absolutely! We use plant-based, biodegradable detergents that are gentle on fabrics and the environment. Our energy-efficient machines and water recycling systems minimize our ecological footprint.

How do you handle delicate fabrics?

Our fabric specialists hand-wash delicate items using specialized techniques. We carefully inspect each garment and follow manufacturer care instructions. All delicate items are air-dried to prevent damage.

What is your pricing structure?

We charge by the bag for wash & fold services. Dry cleaning is priced per item. You'll always receive an upfront quote before we begin cleaning. We offer discounts for recurring service plans.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If you're not completely happy with any item, contact us within 48 hours and we'll re-clean it at no charge. For any damaged items (extremely rare), we have a fair compensation policy.
